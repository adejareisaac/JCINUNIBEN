Who We Are

JCI (Junior Chamber International)

JCI is a global membership-based organization that takes its members through a development journey to equip them with full potential as leaders for a changing world. JCI is a global network of enterprising young leaders, ages 18 to 40. Active in more than 5000 locations across nearly 115 countries. We transform passionate young people into capable and enterprising young leaders through training, programs, projects and events aligned with 4 areas of development opportunity.

JCI Nigeria

JCI Nigeria is the largest youth-based organization in Nigeria with 5000+ active members and alumni network. We equip young people with the tools and resources needed to become well-rounded leaders who can effectively navigate a globalized world and create sustainable changes. Our four areas of opportunity are; Individual development, Business and entrepreneurship, Community action, and International cooperation. Do you want to be a global communicator, entrepreneur, changemaker, or networker? Join us on the journey of developing young leaders for a changing world!

JCI UNIBEN

As a vibrant and dynamic local chapter, JCI UNIBEN serves as the premier platform for students of the University of Benin (UNIBEN) to ignite their leadership potential and drive positive change within their campus and wider community. We provide a unique blend of practical leadership training, entrepreneurial initiatives, and impactful community projects tailored to the specific needs and aspirations of UNIBEN students. Our members are empowered to develop vital skills in communication, project management, and global citizenship, preparing them to excel in their careers and contribute meaningfully to society. JCI UNIBEN is more than an organization; it's a launchpad for future leaders and an inclusive family where every student's voice and vision can thrive. Join us to transform challenges into opportunities and make your mark on the world, starting right here in UNIBEN!

Junior Chamber International UNIBEN is a leadership organization for students committed to creating positive change within and beyond the University of Benin.
